🍄 Lion's Mane Mushroom — Hericium erinaceus

In: Mushroom Extract Complex →

Lion's Mane contains hericenones and erinacines — compounds that stimulate Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) synthesis in the brain. Multiple human clinical trials show improvements in mild cognitive impairment, focus, and memory.

Phytotherapy Research · 2009 · Mori K, et al.

Improving effects of the mushroom Yamabushitake (Hericium erinaceus) on mild cognitive impairment

Subjects taking Lion's Mane for 16 weeks showed significantly higher cognitive function scores vs placebo. Scores declined after discontinuation.

International Journal of Medicinal Mushrooms · 2013 · Lai PL, et al.

Nerve Growth Factor-inducing activity of Hericium erinaceus in human astrocytoma cells

Confirmed that Lion's Mane extracts stimulate NGF synthesis, supporting neuronal growth and protection.

Read on PubMed →
Biomedical Research · 2010 · Nagano M, et al.

Reduction of depression and anxiety by 4 weeks Hericium erinaceus intake

Participants consuming Lion's Mane for 4 weeks reported significantly reduced anxiety and depression compared to the placebo group.

Shilajit supplementation significantly improved altitude sickness symptoms and physical performance in subjects at high altitude.

💧 Methylene Blue — Methylthioninium chloride

In: Methylene Blue Drops →

Methylene Blue is a mitochondrial electron carrier with over 150 years of pharmaceutical use. Modern research shows it acts as an alternative electron acceptor in the mitochondrial electron transport chain, enhancing ATP production and cognitive performance.

Neuroscience · 2004 · Gonzalez-Lima F & Bruchey AK

Methylene blue improves brain oxidative metabolism and memory retention

Low-dose methylene blue significantly enhanced memory retention and brain cytochrome oxidase activity, suggesting improved mitochondrial function.

Read on PubMed →
Progress in Neurobiology · 2009 · Oz M, et al.

Beneficial effects of methylene blue on cognitive health

Methylene blue inhibits tau aggregation and amyloid formation, with potential neuroprotective effects relevant to cognitive health.

Read on PubMed →
Redox Biology · 2012 · Rojas JC, et al.

Methylene blue as a cognitive enhancer

Review confirms methylene blue's role as a metabolic enhancer that improves memory consolidation and reduces oxidative stress at low doses.
